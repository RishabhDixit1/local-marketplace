## App Store / Play Store Basics

- App name: ServiQ
- Subtitle / short description: Local services, quotes, orders, and trusted nearby help.
- Category: Lifestyle or Productivity
- Content rating: No restricted content; user-generated marketplace content moderated by account controls.

## Long Description Draft

ServiQ connects local customers and providers in one mobile workflow. Post a need, discover trusted nearby providers, manage service and product listings, send or accept quotes, complete checkout, and track fulfillment from order detail.

Providers can launch their profile, manage service and product availability, respond to requests, send structured quotes, and receive real-time order and payment updates. Customers can compare nearby help, chat, accept quotes, pay with Razorpay or COD, and follow each task through completion.

## Privacy Notes

- Uses Supabase authentication and profile data for account access.
- Uses Firebase Cloud Messaging for push notifications.
- Uses Firebase Crashlytics and Analytics for stability and product diagnostics.
- Uses Razorpay for payment handoff and verification.
- Does not store payment card details in the app.
