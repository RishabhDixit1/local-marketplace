# ServiQ WhatsApp and Telegram Marketing Campaign Template

## Campaign Goal

Promote ServiQ as a simple, useful local platform:

- For users: convenient, faster help nearby
- For providers: more customers, more income
- For both: local, practical, trustworthy, and easy to try

## App Link

`https://local-marketplace-eta.vercel.app/`

If the production link changes, replace it everywhere before sharing.

## Brand Positioning

ServiQ should not feel like "another app being sold."
It should feel like a practical local solution that helps people get support faster and helps providers grow nearby.

## Message Pillars

- Local help, nearby
- Faster response, less hassle
- Useful for everyday needs
- Better visibility for providers
- Simple experience, real value
- Light feedback ask after trial

## Tone Guidelines

- Keep the message simple and human
- Focus on usefulness, convenience, and local trust
- Avoid hype, pressure, and overpromising
- Avoid hard-selling language
- End with a soft CTA and feedback request

## What To Avoid

- Do not position it like a hard sales ad
- Do not say it is "the best app"
- Do not overpromise results
- Do not make it sound complicated
- Do not focus on download/install language first
- Focus on convenience, speed, nearby help, customers, and income
