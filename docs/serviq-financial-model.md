# REVENUE MODEL

## Revenue Streams

### 1. Transaction Revenue
- Fee: 12% of transaction value
- Avg task: ₹350 × 12% = **₹42 per transaction**
- Target: 500 transactions/day by Month 9

### 2. Premium Subscriptions
- Price: ₹499/month
- Target: 200 providers by Month 6, 1000 by Month 12
- Revenue: ₹499 × 200 = **₹1 Lakh/mo** (Month 6)

### 3. AI Launchpad
- One-time: ₹499
- Target: 100 providers (Month 6), 500 (Month 12)
- Revenue: 100 × ₹499 = **₹50K** (Month 6)

### 4. Priority Boosts
- Per post: ₹25
- 20% of posts are boosted
- Revenue: 500 × 20% × ₹25 = **₹2,500/day**

# APPENDIX: BREAK-EVEN ANALYSIS

## Break-even Calculation

```
Fixed Costs (Monthly):
- Tech: ₹1,40,000
- Team: ₹3,10,000  
- Ops: ₹40,000
Total: ₹4,90,000

Contribution Margin per Transaction:
- Avg transaction: ₹350
- Variable cost (payment): 2%
- Net contribution: ₹350 × 0.98 = ₹343

Break-even transactions: ₹4,90,000 / ₹343 = 1,428 per month
Break-even daily: 1,428 / 30 = 48 transactions/day

Achievable: Month 3 (target 50/day)
```
