# Team Meeting Agenda

**Date:** April 4, 2026  
**Project:** ServiQ  
**Duration:** 30 minutes  
**Goal:** Align on today's highest-impact work, unblock risks quickly, and leave with clear owners for delivery.

## 1. Quick Check-In (5 min)

- Wins since yesterday
- What changed overnight
- Any urgent blocker that needs same-day help

## 2. Current Product Focus (8 min)

- Reconfirm today's core priority: ship a clearer and more trustworthy marketplace experience
- Review the main user journey focus areas:
  - Welcome vs Explore clarity
  - People discovery confidence
  - Tasks follow-through visibility
- Agree what "done today" means for the team

## 3. Must-Fix Issues (7 min)

- Notification deep-link and CTA contract drift
- Product image validation issues
- Any route or state behavior causing user confusion
- Test status for unit coverage and smoke checks

## 4. Build Plan For Today (7 min)

- Add page-context guidance across core dashboard surfaces
- Add compact trust signals on decision-heavy cards
- Improve "what happens next" messaging after key actions
- Tighten overlay and layering consistency where it affects usability

## 5. Decisions, Owners, Next Steps (3 min)

- Confirm owners for each priority item
- Flag dependencies or review needs early
- Capture today's success metric before sign-off

## Expected Outcome

By the end of the meeting, each team member should know:

- what we are shipping today
- what bug or feature they own
- what is blocked
- what we will verify before end of day
