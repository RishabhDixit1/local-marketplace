# ServiQ — Company Overview

---

## 1. Company Overview

ServiQ is a technology-driven hyperlocal marketplace that connects users with nearby service providers and businesses in real time. Through its website and future mobile app, ServiQ makes it easy for users to discover services, communicate instantly, and make decisions based on trust within a defined geographic area.

Founded in 2026, ServiQ aims to digitize local ecosystems. It helps small businesses build their online presence while making it simpler for users to find and interact with nearby services. The platform will evolve through AI-driven personalization, recommendations, and trust systems.

## 2. Core Values (Vision & Mission)

**Vision:**  
To create a unified digital ecosystem where everyone and every local business can build their online identity, visibility, and reputation.

**Mission:**  
To make it easy for people to discover, connect, and transact with nearby service providers through a trusted, real-time hyperlocal platform.

---

## 3. Problem & Solution

**Problem:**  
Local service discovery is fragmented. Users depend on various platforms like search engines, messaging apps, and recommendations, which causes inefficiency, distrust, slow responses, and low visibility. Small businesses face challenges with their online presence, attracting customers, and managing communication.

**Solution:**  
ServiQ provides a single platform that combines:
- Service discovery
- Real-time communication
- Availability tracking
- Trust-based decision-making

This reduces reliance on multiple tools and offers a smooth workflow for users and vendors.
